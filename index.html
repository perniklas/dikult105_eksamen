WELCOME

Google's mobile operating system Android is the biggest competitor to Apple's iOS, but Android is extremely fragmented as a multitude of Original Electronics Manufacturers (OEMs) create a bunch of different devices running their own modified version of Android. As some of these modifications are incredibly bad (looking at you Touchwiz), Google has released its own line of mobile devices that run a pure, stock, unmodified Android for the vanilla Android experience. These devices are what the iPhone is to iOS.
