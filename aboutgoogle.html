GOOGLE

Google is the huge multi-billion dollar company that founded the search engine of the same name and has since then acquired and arguably improved upon a number of services, one of the most popular being YouTube. They offer online internet storage (Drive), online creation and editing of office-related products (Docs, Sheets, Slides), free e-mail with 15GB free storage and a number of other services.

A noteworthy aspect of Google services is both the privacy policies and their transparacy concerning these. Google will gather any and all scraps of information they can, whether it be your location, your recent searches, your favorite bookmarks in their browser along with internet habits, your mail and any use of their products. While this is slightly overwhelming they have publicly stated that this information is only used in order to better serve personalized advertisement and search results.

On the other hand, they do have the power to change that pretty much whenever which is a concern for many. We sincerely hope they continue their work on creating useful tools and services for free. While we, the creators of this website, have no affiliation with Google as a company in any way, we do appreciate when a company creates a good product.
